# Product Brief

## Product Name
Affiliate Flow Studio - Product Hunter

## One-liner
A product scoring and content planning tool for Shopee/TikTok affiliates.

## Main User
Beginner to intermediate affiliate marketers in Thailand.

## Core Problem
Affiliate creators waste time choosing products and creating content that does not convert.

## Core Solution
Give each product a simple score and recommend the best content angle.

## V1 Promise
In under 3 minutes, user can know:
- Is this product worth promoting?
- Who should buy it?
- What angle should I use?
- What hook/caption can I post?
